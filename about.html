Who we are

Luxury&Cars is one of the most exclusive rental companies you can find out there. Unlike every other company, we skip the lowcost and the quantity over quality filosophy and we only work with the best of the best, the super models.And that is what makes us unique. We strongly believe that driving is not only getting from point A to B, is the unique experience that comes with it, specially when you have one of these super cars in your hands. Here we try to enhance that feeling by also providing the most efficient and the fastest service we can. And we treat our clients as well as we do with our cars.

What we do

  • Worldwide car delivery

    Car delivery all over the world with no exceptions. Where ever you want to rent Luxury&Cars will bring you the car. Then, you have many places where you can leave the car, and L&Cars will take care of the rest.

  • Rent a car in our place

    If you feel like coming to us to rent the car or just talk with us, that's another option too. However all of our rental places are currently in Europe. But don't worry, we will be expanding very soon!

  • Chauffeur service

    All our cars can be rent with Chauffeur Service. We guarantee that our drivers speak minimum 2 languages, perfect looking and most important they will care about any detail you may need during your journey.

  • Transport

    If needed, you can just contact us and tell us where you are, and where you want to go. We will bring one of our cars to your location to your entire disposal, or with a Chauffeur. That's up to you.
